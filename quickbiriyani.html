quick biriyani

Ingredients :

Ginger, Garlic, Onion, Coriander Leaves, Mint leaves, Curry Leaves, Green Chilly, Coriander, Red Chillies, Turmeric, Mustard, Fenugreek, Pepper, Cardamom, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Mace.

Preparation :

Marinate Chicken/Meat with Scanna masala saut√© for 5 minutes. Heat the cooker with little oil and put the Chicken/meat in it. Do not add salt. May add a little yogurt with salt if preferred. Start cooking without weight/whistle (4 minutes for chicken, 5 minutes for mutton and 10 minutes for beef). Then add soaked rice and water (1 liter water for 1 kg. rice). Cook with cooker weight till 2 whistles. The quick biriyani is ready. Optional: garnish with cashew raisins and fried onions. Serve with love.
